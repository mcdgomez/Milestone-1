Grade Encoding Guidelines

  1. Grades in the NEW Grading System must be encoded as A,B+,B,C+,C,D,F,FD,WP.
  2. Non FYDP Grades in the OLD Grading System must be from 65-100
  3. FYDP Grades in the NEW Grading System must be encoded as A,B+,B,C+,C,D,F,FD,WP.
  4. FYDP Grades in the OLD Grading System must be encoded as
    O - Outstanding, HS - Highly Satisfactory, S - Satisfactory, MS - Moderately Satisfactory, F - Fair, P - Poor.
  5. NE Grades will not be accepted. Students who have not taken exams must be provided the minimum grade. After the exam has been taken, the teacher should file a grade correction from at the Registrar's office.
  6. Select AUD for Audit students.
